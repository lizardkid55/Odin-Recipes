Margharita Pizza

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 6-12 mins

Total Time: 10-15 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

2 cups of flour

3/4 cups of lukewarm water

1 tsp of active dry yeast

1 1/2 tsp of salt

Directions

1. In a bowl, dissolve the yeast in the water for 5 minutes.

2. Mix in the flour and salt until the mixture is shaggy.

3. Knead the dough with your hands for 5 minutes. Add a teaspoon of flour if the mixture is not easy to work with.

4. Dust your worktop with flour and stretch out your dough into your desired shape, size and thickness. (This recipe will make two 10-inch pizza bases).

5. Add your toppings

6. Place your pizza in the oven for 6-12 minutes at 450 degrees Fahrenheit

7. Enjoy!
